Site Purpose

The purpose of TrackYourStride is to provide runners with valuable resources to help them achieve their fitness goals. The website will offer structured training plans, inspiring stories from fellow runners, and a dynamic calendar of local running events. By providing these services, the site aims to motivate and support runners of all levels, fostering a community centered around the love for running.

Scenarios

  1. Scenario 1: A beginner runner visits the site looking for a training plan to help them prepare for their first 5K race.
    Question: "What training plan should I follow to prepare for my first 5K race?"
    Answer: The user will find a detailed training plan on the "Training Plans" page, tailored for beginners.
  2. Scenario 2: An experienced runner wants to find and participate in local running events.
    Question: "Are there any upcoming running events in my city?"
    Answer: The user can visit the "Events Calendar" page, which dynamically lists upcoming events with dates and locations.
