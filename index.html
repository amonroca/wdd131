São Paulo, Brazil

São Paulo is the largest city in Brazil and the capital of the state of São Paulo. The city is the capital of the state of São Paulo, the most populous Brazilian state. São Paulo is the richest city in Brazil and one of the richest cities in the world. The city has the 10th largest GDP in the world, representing alone 10.7% of all Brazilian GDP and 36% of the production of goods and services in the state of São Paulo, being home to 63% of established multinationals in Brazil, and has been responsible for 28% of the national scientific production in 2005.
